Spicy Thai Basil Chicken

A quick, flavorful, and authentic Thai street food classic that you can make at home in under 30 minutes. Perfect for busy weeknights!

HIGH-PROTEIN QUICK SPICY
Prep 10 min
Cook 15 min
Yields 4 Servings
Calories 350 kcal
[APPETIZING RECIPE IMAGE]
Ingredients
  • 1.5 lbs Ground chicken or thinly sliced chicken breast
  • 1 cup Fresh Thai holy basil leaves
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
Instructions
1
Prepare the Sauce

In a small bowl, whisk together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, and a pinch of sugar. Set aside.

2
Sauté Aromatics

Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil. Add the minced garlic and Thai chilies, stirring constantly for about 30 seconds until fragrant (be careful not to burn the garlic).

3
Cook the Chicken

Add the ground chicken to the pan. Break it apart with a spatula and cook until it is mostly browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

4
Add Sauce and Basil

Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken and toss to coat evenly. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the sauce slightly thickens. Turn off the heat and immediately stir in the fresh basil leaves until they are just wilted.

💡
Chef's Tip Serve immediately over a bed of warm jasmine rice with a crispy fried egg on top for the ultimate authentic experience!
